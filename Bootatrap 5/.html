The master’s thesis is a carefully argued scholarly paper of approximately 15,000 – 17,000 words (roughly 80 pages).

It should present an original argument that is carefully documented from primary and/or secondary sources.The thesis must have a substantial research component and a focus that falls within arts and science, and it must be written under the guidance of an advisor.
